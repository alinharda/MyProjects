Team's Plan

The Robokey team's plan is to spread the term robotics to the Jiu Valley, a small region in Transylvania.

With the popularization of the idea of ​​robotics in this area where coal has been mined in the past at a very high level, the old industry should be replaced by the robotics industry, and the Jiu Valley should become more prosperous and rather a "Robotics Valley".

But we know that it is very unlikely that we will succeed on our own, so we will carry out this project together with our good friends from the other teams: Alphabit RO-137, WAFY RO-063, Digital Thunder TO-171. Because together we can achieve more in a shorter time.

However, as our motto says "UNLOCKING THE FUTURE TOGETHER"

Objectives

Expanding and supporting STEM education, creating a community that encourages exploration.

Building a safe high performance robot through which to develop our technical and design skills.

Creating and sustaining community relationships through education and outreach.

Events

Gala Cercetarii

Our first event was on September 24, 2021 when we were invited by the students from the University of Petrosani to the "Research Gala". We were invited to this gala in a very short time, but we mobilized very quickly and made a functional robot that could lift various objects of low weight.

Medieval Robotics Day

We also participated in a demo organized by the Robotix Hunedoara RO-038 team during an event called "Medieval Robotics Day". During the demo we played some friendly matches, we socialized a lot with the other teams in our county and at the same time we tested our autonomus code.
